    • What are the components of a health campaign?

    01- Planning

    Generate accurate population data &
    create detailed, delivery-level operational
    plans for reaching target populations
    with health interventions

    02-Supply & Logistics

    Manage inventory stock order and
    distribution to ensure appropriate
    availability of commodities to target
    population including reverse logistics

    03-HR/Training

    Manage registry of campaign staff, Train
    staff to carry out their roles and
    responsibilities(registartion, distribution
    and supervision)

    04-Registration

    Ensure all eligible beneficiaries
    /households/ structures are registered
    accuartely

    05-Monitoring

    Monitor campaign planning and
    exxecution to assess effectiveness and
    efficiency of campaign activity and take
    corrective action is needed

    06-Demand Generation

    Generate demand & increase awareness about health intervention by sending the right message via the right channel

    07-Payments

    Track hours worked by the campaign
    staff and ensure timely and transparent
    and release of correct payments

    08-Service Delivery

    Deliver service/ product/ infromation to
    the eligible population(Coverage)
